Iced Tea

Like real iced tea, but do you hate boiling the water and then waiting for the tea to cool? Try making it with hot tap water.

Ingredients

  • 8 tea bags
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 7 1/2 cups cold water

Steps

  1. Put tea bags in a 2 quart pitcher.
  2. Add hot water.
  3. Let sit at least 15 minutes.
  4. Add cold water and remove tea bags.
  5. Refrigerate and serve over ice.
